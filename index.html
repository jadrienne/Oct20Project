More about the Founder

Embarking on the journey of founding Bunnytears, my creative process evolved significantly over the years. Initially, in 2019, I found inspiration in the vast realms of the internet, scrolling through Pinterest and admiring the works of fellow jewelry creators. However, as time passed, I recognized the need for authenticity in my designs. Relying solely on the creations of others began to feel like a limitation, stifling the genuine creative expression I longed for.

A turning point came when I delved deeper into my own imaginative universe. I started to explore the intricate textures and colors of the beads I worked with, finding endless inspiration in their unique characteristics. The beads became my muse, sparking ideas that blended seamlessly with the themes I envisioned in my mind. Moreover, my creative horizons expanded to embrace the vast world of cinema and music. Each film became a canvas of emotions, each melody a symphony of inspiration. From the enchanting narratives of classic movies to the raw energy of punk rock anthems, I discovered a wellspring of creativity waiting to be tapped.

This shift in perspective brought a newfound originality to my designs. Bunnytears ceased to be merely a jewelry brand; it became a canvas where my imagination ran wild, where the fusion of colors, shapes, and themes created pieces that told unique stories. By immersing myself in my own creative essence and drawing from the rich tapestry of life, I found the true essence of Bunnytears‚Äîa brand that echoes my authenticity, passion, and creativity.

Incorporating Stainless Steel Metals

As the founder of Bunnytears, my journey began with a simple desire for quality and longevity in jewelry. In the early days, I used to buy pieces from small businesses, only to be disappointed as they easily rusted, losing their charm over time. This dissatisfaction fueled my determination to create something better. In 2019, when Bunnytears was born, I delved into extensive research and experimentation, particularly focusing on stainless steel materials.

Through meticulous trial and error, I discovered the durability and timeless appeal of stainless steel. Since then, I have embraced this metal in most of my creations, ensuring that every Bunnytears piece not only reflects creativity and style but also stands the test of time, promising enduring beauty to those who wear it.
